# NFC Tuzsa

## **APP DOES NOT HAVE KEYB. SO, IT CANNOT WORK**

NFC Tuzsa is an Android application designed to facilitate balance management and recharging of public transport cards in Spanish cities. With this app, users can easily check their current balance, recharge their transport cards and access relevant information about public transport in their area.


### Main features

Balance check: Users can check the current balance of their transport card in real time.
Fast reloads: Allows users to reload balance on their transport cards quickly and conveniently.

Multi-city compatibility: The application is compatible with public transport cards from various Spanish cities like Zaragoza or Sevilla.

Transport information: Provides up-to-date information on routes, schedules and fares of public transport in the supported cities.
Installation
The NFC Tuzsa application is available for Android devices.


### Use

Balance inquiry: Once logged in, users can check the balance of their transportation card by selecting the corresponding option in the main menu.
